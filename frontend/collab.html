CALLING ALL THE LOCAL THRIFT STORES!!

Ready to shake up fashion with us? Let's make sustainable style the new cool.



Why Collaborate with Us?

  • Boost Your Visibility: Get your amazing pieces in front of our fashion-obsessed Gen Z community - they're always hunting for the perfect thrift find.
  • Promote Sustainability: Let's team up to make sustainable fashion the norm, not the exception. Less waste, more style!
  • Empower Local Businesses: You're the heart of what we do - local thrift stores bringing personality and uniqueness to fashion that big chains just can't match.

What We're Looking For

We're pretty picky (in the best way!) about what goes on our platform. Here's what catches our eye:

Quality Check

  • Condition: No stains, rips, or that "I've lived a thousand lives" look - we want pieces that still have stories to tell.
  • Cleanliness: Fresh and clean - because nobody wants mystery smells with their vintage finds.
  • Functionality: Zippers that zip, buttons that button - you know, the basics that actually work.

Style Vibes

  • Current Trends: If Gen Z is wearing it, we want it. Think Y2K, cottagecore, dark academia - all the aesthetics that are having their moment.
  • Unique Pieces: That one-off vintage jacket or those pants nobody else has? Yes please!
  • Mix It Up: From casual coffee-run outfits to "I'm going to a gallery opening" looks - variety is the spice of thrift.

Planet-Friendly Bonus Points

  • Eco-Friendly Fabrics: Organic cotton, linen, recycled materials - Mother Earth says thank you.
  • Less Plastic Please: When you're sending stuff over, skip the plastic wrap. The planet (and we) appreciate it.

How to Get Started

  • Hit Us Up: Fill out our collaboration form or drop us a line at aestheticize@gmail.com
  • Show & Tell: Send us some photos and tell us about what you've got - we love a good backstory.
  • We'll Get Back to You: Give us about a week to review everything and we'll let you know what's up.
  • Shipping Made Simple: If we're vibing with your pieces, we'll sort out how to get them to us - whether that's shipping or a local pickup.

The Fine Print:

  • All deals are final - no take-backs on store credit.
  • Slot booking fees are non-refundable (sorry, but we've got to cover our costs).
  • Our team makes the final call on what works - trust us, we know our crowd.
  • Store credit is exclusive to us - can't be mixed with other discounts.
